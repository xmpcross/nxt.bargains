FAQs

What features should I look for in a smartwatch?

Consider features like health monitoring, fitness tracking, battery life, compatibility with your smartphone, and additional functionalities such as music storage or GPS.

Are smartwatches waterproof?

Many smartwatches, like the Garmin Forerunner 245 and Samsung Galaxy Watch 5, are water-resistant, making them suitable for swimming or workouts in the rain. Always check the specific device's water resistance rating.

How long do smartwatch batteries last?

Battery life varies by model. For instance, the Suunto 9 Peak Pro boasts exceptional battery life, while others like the Apple Watch Series 8 may require more frequent charging.

Can I make calls with my smartwatch?

Yes, many smartwatches, especially those from Apple and Samsung, allow you to make and receive calls directly from your wrist if paired with a compatible smartphone.

Do I need a smartphone to use a smartwatch?

While some functionalities may work independently, most smartwatches offer enhanced features when paired with a smartphone, such as notifications and app integrations.
